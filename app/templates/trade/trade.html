{# How it works — collapsible #}

Buying

Place a buy order with your maximum price per unit. The total cost is escrowed (held) from your money balance immediately. If a matching sell order exists at or below your price, the trade fills instantly. Otherwise your order sits on the book until a seller matches it.

Selling

Place a sell order with your minimum price. The resources are escrowed from your stockpile. If a matching buy order exists at or above your price, the trade fills instantly. A {{ fee_pct }}% fee is deducted from the sale proceeds.

Order Book

The order book shows the top 10 buy and sell price levels for the selected resource. Green rows are bids (buyers); red rows are asks (sellers). The spread is the gap between the best bid and best ask.

Cancelling

Open orders can be cancelled from the "My Orders" tab. Escrowed money or resources are refunded in full when you cancel.
