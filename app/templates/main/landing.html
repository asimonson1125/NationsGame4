Explore & Expand

Claim territory across five continents — from the frozen peaks of Tind to the deserts of Zaheria. Each region offers unique terrain and natural resources.

Build Your Economy

Construct over 60 types of factories. Manage supply chains from raw materials to advanced goods. Balance inputs, outputs, and production capacity.

Military & Combat

Recruit units, organise divisions, and equip them with randomised loot-crate gear. Battle other players or fight PvE encounters for rewards.

Global Market

Trade commodities and natural resources on a player-driven market. Set buy and sell orders, track price history, and profit from the spread.

Alliances

Join forces with other nations. Form alliances for mutual defence, coordinate strategy, and climb the leaderboards together.

Compete & Rise

Earn Greatness Points from population, land, factories, buildings, and military. Compete on global and continental leaderboards.
